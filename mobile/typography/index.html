The typeface we use throughout the Thrive platform is Halyard Display, by Darden Studio.

Modular Page Styles

The following typographic styles are used on modular pages within the mobile apps, including the various headings, paragraphs, links, and blockquotes. Line height should be set at 1.3em for all headings and paragraphs.

  • Modular Page Heading One
    The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog
    Size: 27px Weight: Bold Letter-Spacing: 0px
  • Modular Page Heading Two
    The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog
    Size: 21px Weight: Medium Letter-Spacing: 0
  • Modular Page Heading Three
    The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog
    Size: 18px Weight: Medium Letter-Spacing: 0px
  • Heading Number Four
    The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog
    Size: 13px Weight: Semibold Letter-Spacing: 0.8px
  • Modular Page Paragraph
    The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog
    Size: 17px Weight: Regular Letter-Spacing: 0px
  • Modular Page Blockquote
    "The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog"
    Size: 17px Weight: Book Letter-Spacing: 0
  • Modular Page Image Caption
    The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog
    Size: 14px Weight: Book Letter-Spacing: 0
